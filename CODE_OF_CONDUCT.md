# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this repository a welcoming, respectful, and useful place for learners, engineers, architects, and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still correcting inaccuracies.
- Help beginners without gatekeeping.
- Discuss tradeoffs with evidence.
- Credit sources and link official documentation where appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing credentials, secrets, private account information, or confidential architecture details.
- Promoting unsafe production practices without warnings.
- Copying proprietary or copyrighted material without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked participation.
