## How To Use Comparison Tables

- Use mappings to translate prior AWS, Azure, or GCP knowledge into Alibaba Cloud vocabulary.
- Do not assume services have identical limits, pricing, or feature behavior.
- Validate security, networking, logging, and regional support before adopting a mapping.
