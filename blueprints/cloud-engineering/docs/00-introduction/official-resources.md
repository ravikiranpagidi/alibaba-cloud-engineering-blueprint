## Verification Checklist

- Confirm the service is available in your target region.
- Confirm quotas and request increases early.
- Confirm pricing dimensions, billing model, and data transfer charges.
- Confirm whether features are generally available, preview, or limited access.
- Confirm compliance and data residency requirements with your organization.
